/*========= Description =========*/

WP-bs-theme-simple is a simple, minimal, clean, and responsive WordPress theme, well suited for a variety of application possibilities. Use the Theme Options inside your Customizer to modify the theme for your needs. Its responsive design is based on Bootstrap 4, so your page will always look fine on any device. The theme also comes with social icon integration, using the fantastic fonts from font-awesome.

/*========= Installation =========*/

In your admin panel, go to Appearance -> Themes and click the 'Add New' button.

Type in WP-bs-theme-simple in the search form and press the 'Enter' key on your keyboard.

Click on the 'Activate' button to use your new theme right away.

Afterwards navigate to Appearance > Customize in your admin panel and customize to taste.
